Executive Summary
Strategic Overview

Q1 Performance Highlights Sample Copy

  • NCTRCA serves as the premier regional certification authority for North Central Texas, upholding the highest standards of integrity and compliance with federal, regional, and local regulations.
  • NCTRCA certifies seven business designations: DBE, ACDBE, MBE, WBE, SBE, SBEC, and SBE-PLUS each providing access to procurement, contracting, and bid opportunities across the DFW metroplex.
  • Effective May 9, 2024, the U.S. Department of Transportation implemented significant updates to the DBE and ACDBE programs. Certified firms are encouraged to review the USDOT Final Rule and the NCTRCA Important Notice.
  • NCTRCA's rigorous verification process confirms that applicant firms own, manage, and control operations of for-profit businesses ensuring fair competition and institutional credibility.
  • NCTRCA is a 501(c)(3) non-profit and government entity committed to building a future where economic opportunity is not just available but accessible.

Capital Access & Growth Programs
Funding & Grant Opportunities

Grants, loans, and bonding programs available to NCTRCA-certified businesses in North Central Texas. These resources are designed to help you access capital, scale operations, and compete for larger contracts. Explore all resources →

SBA Loan Program
SBA 7(a) Loan Program

The SBA's primary lending program offers loans up to $5 million for certified small businesses. Funds can be used for working capital, equipment, real estate, or business expansion. NCTRCA-certified firms often qualify.

Learn More →
Dallas County Program
BCL of Texas: Dallas Small Business Diversity Fund

Backed by Comerica Bank, this fund provides capital specifically for minority-owned and underserved businesses in Dallas County. Designed to level the playing field for BIPOC and women-owned firms seeking growth capital.

Learn More →
Women-Owned Business Grant
Amber Grant: Monthly $10,000 Awards

WBE-certified businesses are encouraged to apply for monthly $10,000 Amber Grants. Year-end recipients are eligible for an additional $25,000 award. Simple two-question application. Applications close at end of each month.

Apply Now →
SBA Bonding Assistance
SBA Surety Bond Guarantee Program

A top request from NCTRCA members. This SBA program helps certified small businesses obtain the bonding needed to bid on and win government and private contracts that would otherwise be out of reach.

Learn More →
Goldman Sachs Program
10,000 Small Businesses at Dallas College

NCTRCA partner Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses, offered through Dallas College, provides business education, mentorship, and access to capital networks for certified small business owners in North Texas.

Learn More →
SBA Microloan
SBA Microloan Program: Up to $50,000

For early-stage certified businesses, SBA Microloans offer accessible financing up to $50,000 for working capital, inventory, supplies, and equipment. More accessible than traditional bank loans with flexible terms.

Learn More →
